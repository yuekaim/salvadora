ABOUT

Salvadora McCaffrey is a transdisciplinary artist and designer native to Boston. Through the mergence of various mediums, she creates spaces and objects for experiences. Public(s), and their engagement, are tapped into to both communicate and socially experiment. Spatial theories, biographical histories, and sociopolitical excavations run amok in her work. McCaffrey is an alumna of Boston Arts Academy and is currently pursuing her BFA at the School of The Museum of Fine Arts at Tufts University.
